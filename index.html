About Me



Josue Nunez

Engineer

I have over 6.5 years of experience as a design engineer in the oil & gas industry. My experience does not only include developing and launching downhole drilling tools, but also sustaining, improving, and championing the company’s product portfolio selling to both internal and external customers.

Once the fundamentals are there, the best way to learn is by delving in. I know that, if given the opportunity, I can be a great asset to you and all the stakeholders by attaining results that matter. If you would like to see the difference that this life long learner can make lets have a chat.

Professional Experience



  • 2020-2019

    Regional Design Engineer

    Team lead on new NOV drill bit designs. Product was designed off of customer provided requirements using 3D modeling software. Led team meetings between field engineers, product line and sales managers to improve upon the current top performer in the region. After conferring, design was manufactured and shipped out to the drilling rig.

  • 2018-2016

    Regional Product Engineer

    Took part in transition a product line from Houston to Louisiana. During the transition, I was head engineer in training the new personnel to develop and maintain NOV’s 3D Anderreamer. Successfully troubleshooted drilling tools to meet the sudden shipment dates. Designed handling tools as required to reduce assembly time and reduce costs.

  • 2016-2015

    Product Line Engineer

    Successfully merged and updated an acquired product line into NOV’s current coring product portfolio.

  • 2013-2015

    New Product Development Engineer

    Worked with a handpicked engineering team to develop the next generation electronically activated downhole underreamer. After design was ready for a prototype, it was manufactured, assembled, and field tested for design validation before commercializing. Created the 3D models and 2D drawings using Solidworks.
